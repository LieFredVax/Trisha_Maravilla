Nursing Interventions:

  • Ensure proper storage and handling of vaccines according to manufacturer's recommendations.
  • Educate parents/caregivers about the importance of vaccination and potential side effects.
  • Assess for contraindications and allergies before administering vaccines.
  • Administer vaccines using appropriate techniques and follow recommended schedules.
  • Monitor for adverse reactions post-vaccination and provide appropriate care as needed.
  • Maintain accurate documentation of vaccines administered and follow-up schedules.
